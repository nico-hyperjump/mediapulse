## Overview

This document outlines the comprehensive development plan for the AI-Powered Stock Newsletter System throughout 2026. The plan is organized into four quarters, each containing 2-3 milestones with significant, measurable deliverables.

## Plan Structure

The development plan is organized into the following sections:

- **[Q1 2026: Foundation & Core Infrastructure](/plan/q1-foundation)** - Project setup, database foundation, agent framework, and web dashboard
- **[Q2 2026: Data Collection & Entity Intelligence](/plan/q2-data-collection)** - Query strategy agent, data collection from multiple sources, and advanced features
- **[Q3 2026: Analysis & Content Generation](/plan/q3-analysis-content)** - Analysis agent, content generation, and quality assurance (Final Development Milestone)
- **[Q4 2026: Maintenance & Production Operations](/plan/q4-delivery-production)** - Delivery system, scheduler, learning agent, and production maintenance
- **[Success Metrics & KPIs](/plan/success-metrics)** - Technical and business metrics for measuring success
- **[Risk Mitigation](/plan/risk-mitigation)** - Technical and business risks with mitigation strategies
- **[Dependencies & Prerequisites](/plan/dependencies)** - External services and team requirements
- **[Review Process](/plan/review-process)** - Quarterly review process and notes
